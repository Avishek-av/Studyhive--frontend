GENERAL

About StudyHive

Yes! StudyHive is completely free for all students. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone.

StudyHive follows the NEB (National Examination Board) curriculum for Grade 11 and 12 in Nepal. All content is aligned with the latest syllabus.

StudyHive was created by students who experienced the challenges of exam preparation firsthand. We built the platform we wished we had during our studies.

Unlike YouTube, StudyHive offers a structured, distraction-free learning experience. No ads, no recommended videos pulling your attention. Just focused content aligned exactly with your syllabus, with progress tracking and organized learning paths.

Heat and Work

Heat (Q) is energy transfer due to temperature difference.

Work (W) is energy transfer by mechanical means.

Sign Convention
+Q: into system, +W: by system
Internal Energy (U)
Sum of all microscopic energies

First Law of Thermodynamics

First Law (Energy Conservation)

ŒîU = Q - W

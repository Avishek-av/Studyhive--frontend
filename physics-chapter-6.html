Progressive Waves

A progressive wave is a wave that travels through a medium, transferring energy from one point to another without permanent displacement of the medium.

Transverse Waves
Particles vibrate perpendicular to wave direction (e.g., light, water ripples)
Longitudinal Waves
Particles vibrate parallel to wave direction (e.g., sound, springs)

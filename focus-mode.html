Focus Mode Active

In Focus Mode, all distractions fade away. The navigation hides, colors dim, and only your study content remains visible. This helps you concentrate better and retain more.

HOW IT WORKS

What Focus Mode Does

Hides Navigation

The header and sidebar disappear, giving you a clean view of just your study content.

Dims Surroundings

Background elements fade to a calm, dark overlay so your eyes focus on the text.

Reduces Visual Noise

Unnecessary elements are hidden, leaving only what you need to read and learn.

No Notifications

All pop-ups and floating elements are suppressed during your study session.

WHY IT MATTERS

Benefits of Focused Study

  • Better Retention - Studies show focused study sessions improve memory retention by up to 50%
  • Faster Learning - Without distractions, you cover more material in less time
  • Reduced Eye Strain - Calm, dark interface is easier on your eyes during long sessions
  • Deeper Understanding - Concentrated attention helps you grasp complex concepts

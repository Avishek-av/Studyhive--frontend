Physical and Chemical Properties

Physical State
Lower amines are gases (methylamine) or liquids with fishy odor. Higher amines are solids.
Solubility
Lower amines are soluble in water due to hydrogen bonding. Solubility decreases with increase in molecular weight.
Boiling Points
Primary > Secondary > Tertiary (for same molecular weight) due to hydrogen bonding.
Basicity
Amines are basic due to lone pair on nitrogen. Order: Secondary > Primary > Tertiary > Ammonia (in aqueous solution).

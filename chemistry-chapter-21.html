Study of atomic nuclei, radioactive decay, and nuclear reactions.

Radioactivity

Definition: Spontaneous emission of radiation from unstable nuclei.

Discovery: Becquerel (1896), studied by Marie & Pierre Curie.

Natural Radioactivity
Uranium, thorium, radium series
Artificial Radioactivity

Harmful Effects

Biological Effects:

  • Radiation sickness
  • Genetic mutations
  • Cancer induction
  • Organ damage

Environmental Effects:

  • Radioactive contamination
  • Nuclear waste disposal

Protection: Shielding, distance, time limitation, monitoring.
